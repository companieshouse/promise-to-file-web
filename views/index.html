Tell us if a company is still required

Use this service to tell us if you still require a company that has overdue filing.

This will temporarily stop us from closing it on your behalf if we think it is not operational. The company must still file, and it will receive a fine for filing late that increases over time.

If the company is not required, you should close the company.
